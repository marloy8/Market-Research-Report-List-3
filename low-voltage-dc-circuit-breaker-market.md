<p><p>The Low Voltage DC Circuit Breaker market consists of two main types - Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB) and Molded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB). On the other hand, MCCBs are larger and more robust circuit breakers commonly used in industrial and commercial settings to protect against higher levels of current.
