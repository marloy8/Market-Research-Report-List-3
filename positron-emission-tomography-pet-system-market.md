<p><p>The Positron Emission Tomography (PET) System Market Types include Direct Imaging, which involves directly detecting the annihilation photons produced by positron-emitting radiotracers. Indirect Imaging utilizes a scintillation crystal to convert annihilation photons into light, which is then detected by photodetectors. Alternative Imaging methods involve novel approaches such as using time-of-flight information or iterative reconstruction algorithms to improve image quality and reduce artifacts in PET scans. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences in the medical imaging industry.</p></p>
