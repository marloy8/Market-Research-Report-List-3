<p><ul><li>Polyurethane</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floor sealers come in different types, including polyurethane, solvent-based, and water-based. Polyurethane sealers are durable and provide a strong protective layer against moisture and chemicals, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Solvent-based sealers contain volatile organic compounds and are known for their fast drying time and strong odor. Water-based sealers are environmentally friendly and have low VOC content, making them safer to use indoors. Each type of floor sealer caters to specific needs and preferences of consumers in the market.</p></p>
