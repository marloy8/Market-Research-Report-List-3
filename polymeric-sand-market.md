<p><strong>The Polymeric Sand Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymeric Sand</li><li>Polymeric Dust</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymeric sand is a type of sand mixed with additives like silica that are activated when water is applied to create a binding agent between pavers. It helps to prevent weed growth and erosion while providing a more stable surface for outdoor spaces. Polymeric dust, on the other hand, is a fine powder version of polymeric sand that is used to fill in the joints between pavers. Both products offer similar benefits in terms of enhancing the durability and longevity of outdoor hardscape surfaces.</p></p>
