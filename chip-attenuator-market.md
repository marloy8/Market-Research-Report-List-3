<p><p>Chip attenuators are passive electronic components used to reduce the power of a signal without significantly distorting it. They come in various types, including temperature stable and temperature variable attenuators. Temperature stable attenuators maintain their attenuation levels consistently across a wide temperature range, making them ideal for applications where temperature fluctuations are common. On the other hand, temperature variable attenuators are designed to vary their attenuation levels based on changes in temperature, providing flexibility in certain applications.</p></p>
