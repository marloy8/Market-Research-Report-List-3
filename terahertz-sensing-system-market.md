<p><p>Terahertz sensing systems are utilized in both photonic sensing and electronic sensing markets. Photonic sensing involves the use of light to detect and analyze terahertz waves, providing high precision and sensitivity. On the other hand, electronic sensing relies on electronic components such as field-effect transistors to detect terahertz signals, offering faster response times and the ability to operate in harsh environments. Both markets cater to different applications and industries, with photonic sensing being more prevalent in research and medical fields, while electronic sensing is commonly used in security and industrial applications.</p></p>
