<p><strong>The Electrical Steel Strips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel Strips</li><li>Non-oriented Electrical Steel Strips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical steel strips are categorized into two main types: oriented electrical steel strips and non-oriented electrical steel strips. Oriented electrical steel strips have a unique crystalline structure that allows them to be magnetized in a specific direction, making them ideal for applications such as transformers and electric motors. Non-oriented electrical steel strips, on the other hand, have a random crystalline structure that allows for magnetic properties in all directions, making them suitable for applications like rotating machinery and small transformers.</p></p>
