<p><p>Lab Automation involves the use of technology to streamline laboratory processes. Task Targeted Automation (TTA) focuses on automating specific tasks within a lab, such as sample preparation or data analysis. Total Laboratory Automation (TLA) takes this a step further by fully automating all aspects of the lab, from sample handling to result reporting. TTA is more common in smaller labs with specific needs, while TLA is popular in larger labs looking to increase efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
