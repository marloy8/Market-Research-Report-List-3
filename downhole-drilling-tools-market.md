<p><ul><li>Fixed Cutter Drill Bit</li><li>Roller Cone Drill Bit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Downhole Drilling Tools Market consists of two main types of drill bits: Fixed Cutter Drill Bit and Roller Cone Drill Bit. Fixed Cutter Drill Bits have blades that do not move and are designed for drilling through hard formations such as granite or hard sandstone. Roller Cone Drill Bits have cone-shaped rollers with teeth that rotate and are ideal for drilling through softer formations like limestone or shale. These drill bits are essential tools used in the oil and gas industry for drilling wells efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
