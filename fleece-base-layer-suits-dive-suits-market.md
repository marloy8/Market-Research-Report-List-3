<p><p>Fleece base layer suits, also known as dive suits, come in three main types: layer top, layer pant, and layer full. Layer top suits cover the upper body, providing warmth and insulation for divers. Layer pant suits cover the lower body, protecting divers' legs from cold water temperatures. Layer full suits cover the entire body, offering complete warmth and protection for divers in chilly diving conditions. These suits are essential for maintaining body heat while diving in cold waters.</p></p>
