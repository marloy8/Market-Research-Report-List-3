<p><p>Gluten free products are food and beverages that do not contain gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. These products are specifically catered to individuals with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, which is an autoimmune disorder triggered by the ingestion of gluten. Gluten free products include a wide range of items such as breads, pastas, snacks, and beverages that are made without gluten-containing ingredients.</p><p>The Gluten Free Products Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.3% during the forecast period. The market growth is primarily driven by the increasing prevalence of gluten sensitivities and celiac disease, as well as growing awareness about the health benefits of gluten free diets. Consumers are increasingly seeking out gluten free alternatives as part of a healthy lifestyle and dietary choice.</p><p>The latest trends in the Gluten Free Products Market include the introduction of innovative gluten free products that are not only free from gluten but also high in nutritional value. Manufacturers are also focusing on improving the taste and texture of gluten free products to cater to a wider consumer base. Additionally, the use of alternative grains and ingredients such as quinoa, buckwheat, and chickpea flour in gluten free products is gaining popularity among health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
