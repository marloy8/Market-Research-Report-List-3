<p><ul><li>Straight Shank</li><li>Taper Shank</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Replaceable tip drills are categorized into three market types based on their shank designs: straight shank, taper shank, and others. Straight shank drills have a uniform diameter along their length, making them suitable for use in standard drill chucks. Taper shank drills gradually increase in diameter towards the cutting end, allowing for a secure fit in tapered drill press spindles. The "others" category includes unique shank designs not covered by the previous two types, offering versatility for specific applications.</p></p>
