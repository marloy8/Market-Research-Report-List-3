<p><p>Copper Ion Selective Electrodes are commonly categorized into two main types: Liquid Membrane and PVC Membrane. Liquid Membrane electrodes utilize a liquid membrane impregnated with ionophores to selectively detect copper ions, while PVC Membrane electrodes consist of a polyvinyl chloride membrane containing selective ionophores. Both types of electrodes offer accurate and specific detection of copper ions in various samples, making them essential tools in industries such as environmental monitoring, food testing, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
