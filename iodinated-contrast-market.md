<p><p>Iodinated Contrast market types refer to different imaging techniques that utilize contrast agents containing iodine. X-ray/CT contrast agents are used in imaging procedures to enhance visibility of blood vessels and tissues. MRI contrast agents containing iodine are used to improve visibility of certain tissues or organs in magnetic resonance imaging. Ultrasound contrast agents with iodine are used to enhance visualization of blood flow and tissue structures.
