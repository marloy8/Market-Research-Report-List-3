<p><p>PETG Sheets are available in different types based on their manufacturing process and application. Extruded Grade PETG Sheets are produced through extrusion process and are suitable for applications requiring high transparency and impact resistance. Injection Molding Grade PETG Sheets are designed for use in injection molding processes, offering excellent dimensional stability and surface finish. Blow Molding Grade PETG Sheets are specifically formulated for blow molding applications, providing enhanced stretchability and ease of processing. Each type caters to specific market needs and requirements.</p></p>
