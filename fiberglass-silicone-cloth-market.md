<p><p>Fiberglass silicone cloth is available in two main types: one-sided coating and double-sided coating. One-sided coating refers to a fiberglass cloth that has silicone coating applied to only one side, providing heat resistance and durability. Double-sided coating involves silicone coating on both sides of the fiberglass cloth, enhancing its resistance to high temperatures and making it suitable for various applications. Both types of coatings offer protective properties and are used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
