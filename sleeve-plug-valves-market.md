<p><p>Sleeve plug valves come in different types based on the number of ways they can control the flow of media. Two way plug valves have two ports for regulating the flow in a straight line. Three way plug valves have three ports for diverting flow or mixing media. Multiway plug valves have multiple ports for complex flow control applications. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the desired function and application requirements.</p></p>
