<p><p>Glass free 3D displays are becoming increasingly popular in the market, with two main types dominating the industry - Parallax Barrier Technology and Lenticular Display Technology. Parallax Barrier Technology works by placing a barrier in front of the display that blocks the light from reaching the viewer's eyes, creating a 3D effect. Lenticular Display Technology uses a series of lenses to direct light in different directions, creating the illusion of depth. Both technologies are used to provide immersive 3D experiences without the need for glasses.</p></p>
