<p><p>Inhalation capsules are a type of drug delivery system that allows for the direct administration of medication into the lungs through inhalation. There are two main types of inhalation capsules on the market - gelatin capsules and hypromellose capsules. Gelatin capsules are made from animal-derived gelatin and are commonly used for inhalation of dry powder medications. Hypromellose capsules, on the other hand, are made from plant-derived cellulose and are often used for inhalation of liquid medications. Both types of capsules are designed to provide effective and targeted delivery of medication to the lungs.</p></p>
