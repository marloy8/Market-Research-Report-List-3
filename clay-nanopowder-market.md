<p><p>Clay Nanopowder Market can be categorized into two main types: Kaolinite and Smectite. Kaolinite is a type of clay nanopowder that is derived from kaolin clay minerals and is known for its high heat resistance and strength. Smectite, on the other hand, is another type of clay nanopowder that is characterized by its high water absorption capacity and ability to swell. Both Kaolinite and Smectite markets have their own unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
