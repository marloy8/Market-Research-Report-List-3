<p><p>Manual welders can be classified into different market types such as projection welding and spot welding. Projection welding is commonly used for joining larger pieces of metal together, while spot welding is ideal for joining two or more metal sheets together at specific points. Both types of welders require manual operation which gives the operator more control over the welding process. Projection welding is popular in industries such as automotive and construction, while spot welding is commonly used in the manufacturing of appliances and electronics.</p></p>
