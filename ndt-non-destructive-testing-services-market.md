<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services market types include Ultrasonic, Radiographic, Eddy Current, Magnetic Particle, Acoustic Emission, and Terahertz Imaging. Ultrasonic testing uses high frequency sound waves to detect flaws in materials, while Radiographic testing uses X-rays or gamma rays. Eddy Current testing measures conductivity changes in materials, Magnetic Particle testing detects surface and near-surface flaws, Acoustic Emission testing monitors stress-related defects, and Terahertz Imaging uses electromagnetic radiation for non-invasive inspection. These methods provide crucial information without causing damage to the tested materials.</p></p>
