<p><p>The Automotive Diesel Fuel Injection System market consists of three main types: Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System, Common Rail Injection System, and Other Injection Systems. Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System involves delivering fuel from the pump to the injectors through individual lines. Common Rail Injection System utilizes a high-pressure pump to supply fuel to a common rail that then distributes it to the injectors. Other Injection Systems includes various alternative designs and technologies for fuel injection in diesel engines.</p></p>
