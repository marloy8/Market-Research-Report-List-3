<p><p>The commercial vehicle gasoline engine intake valve market consists of three main types: mono metallic valve, bimetallic valve, and hollow valve. Mono metallic valves are made entirely of one material, typically steel or titanium, offering durability and strength. Bimetallic valves combine two different materials, such as steel and nickel, to achieve a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. Hollow valves have a hollow core to reduce weight and improve airflow. Each type has its own unique advantages for commercial vehicle gasoline engines.</p></p>
