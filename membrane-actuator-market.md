<p><p>Membrane actuators are classified into different types based on the mechanism used to actuate the membrane. The pneumatic membrane actuator utilizes air pressure to flex the membrane, providing precise control and responsiveness. On the other hand, the electric membrane actuator relies on electrical signals to deform the membrane. There are also other types of membrane actuators that may utilize different methods such as hydraulic or mechanical mechanisms to actuate the membrane. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
