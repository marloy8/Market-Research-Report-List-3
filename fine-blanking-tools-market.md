<p><p>Fine blanking tools are used for precision cutting of intricate shapes in metal components. Both types of tools are essential in the manufacturing industry for producing high-quality metal parts with tight tolerances and minimal burrs. Each type has its own advantages and applications depending on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
