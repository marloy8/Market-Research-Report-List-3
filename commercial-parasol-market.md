<p><p>Commercial parasol market types include centre pole parasols, cantilever parasols, and wall-mounted parasols. Centre pole parasols are supported by a single pole in the center, providing shade for outdoor seating areas. Cantilever parasols have an off-set design with the pole to allow for more flexibility in positioning and shading. Wall-mounted parasols are attached directly to walls or structures, ideal for spaces with limited floor space. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the commercial market for outdoor shading solutions.</p></p>
