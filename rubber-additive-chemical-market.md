<p><p>Rubber additive chemicals are classified into different types based on their functions in the rubber industry. Rubber antioxidants are used to prevent rubber materials from degradation due to exposure to oxygen and heat. Rubber accelerators promote the vulcanization process, improving the strength and elasticity of rubber products. Other rubber additives include antiozonants, blowing agents, and adhesion promoters that serve various purposes in enhancing the performance and durability of rubber materials. These additives play a crucial role in optimizing the properties of rubber products for different applications.</p></p>
