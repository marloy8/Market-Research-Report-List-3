<p><p>Portable water purifiers are available in various types such as reverse osmosis (RO), ion exchange, filtration, and distillation. RO purifiers use pressure to filter out impurities, ion exchange systems swap ions to remove contaminants, filtration systems use a physical barrier to trap pollutants, and distillation involves boiling water and collecting the steam to remove impurities. Each type offers unique benefits and is suitable for different water purification needs, ensuring access to clean and safe drinking water in various settings.</p></p>
