<p><p>High Speed Torque Angle Sensor market offers two main types: Potentiometer Torque Sensor and Non-contact Torque Sensor. Potentiometer Torque Sensor uses a rotating shaft to measure torque by changes in resistance, making it durable and cost-effective. On the other hand, Non-contact Torque Sensor employs magnetic or optical technology to measure torque without physical contact, ensuring high accuracy and minimal wear and tear. Both types cater to different needs and applications in industries requiring precise torque angle measurements at high speeds.</p></p>
