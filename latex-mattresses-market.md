<p><p>Latex mattresses can be categorized into two main types: artificial latex mattresses and natural latex mattresses. Artificial latex mattresses are made using synthetic latex, which is a man-made material that mimics the properties of natural latex. On the other hand, natural latex mattresses are crafted from the sap of rubber trees, making them a more eco-friendly and sustainable option.
