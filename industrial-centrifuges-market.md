<p><p>Industrial centrifuges are used to separate components of a heterogeneous mixture by spinning it at high speeds. There are two main types of industrial centrifuges - sedimentation centrifuges and filtering centrifuges. Sedimentation centrifuges rely on the force of gravity to separate solids from liquids, while filtering centrifuges use a filter media to separate components based on their size. Both types of centrifuges are essential in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and wastewater treatment for efficient separation and purification processes.</p></p>
