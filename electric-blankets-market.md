<p><p>Electric blankets are divided into two main market types: underblankets and overblankets. Underblankets are placed on top of the mattress and underneath the fitted sheet, providing warmth from below. Overblankets, on the other hand, are placed on top of the user, providing warmth directly on top of the body. Both types of electric blankets offer a convenient and efficient way to stay warm during cold nights, with various features and options available to suit individual preferences and needs.</p></p>
