<p><p>Sintering furnaces are categorized into several types based on their method of heat generation. Electric sintering furnaces use electric coils to generate heat, combustion sintering furnaces use flames from burning fuel, microwave sintering furnaces use electromagnetic waves to heat the material, steam sintering furnaces use steam as a heat source, and other sintering furnaces utilize different heat generation methods such as induction heating. Each type of sintering furnace has its own advantages and applications depending on the specific requirements of the sintering process.</p></p>
