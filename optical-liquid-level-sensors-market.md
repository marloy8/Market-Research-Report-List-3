<p><ul><li>Digital Optical Liquid Level Sensors</li><li>Analog Optical Liquid Level Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Liquid Level Sensors are categorized into two main types: Digital and Analog. Digital Optical Liquid Level Sensors utilize on/off signals to indicate the presence or absence of liquid. They are easy to integrate into digital control systems for accurate level monitoring. Analog Optical Liquid Level Sensors, on the other hand, provide continuous output signals that vary based on the liquid level. They are suitable for applications that require precise and real-time monitoring of liquid levels. Both types offer reliable and efficient solutions for liquid level sensing in various industries.</p></p>
