<p><p>Silver based antimicrobials are used in various industries for their effective antibacterial properties. They are often categorized based on the carriers they are attached to, such as silicate carriers, phosphate carriers, titanium dioxide carriers, and glass carriers. Silicate carriers offer stability and controlled release of silver ions, phosphate carriers provide enhanced dispersion, titanium dioxide carriers offer photocatalytic properties, and glass carriers provide durability. Each type of carrier offers unique advantages in different applications, allowing for versatile use of silver based antimicrobials in various industries.</p></p>
