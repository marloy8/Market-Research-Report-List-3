<p><strong>The Ultrasound Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Mode</li><li>B-Mode or 2D Mode</li><li>C-Mode</li><li>M-Mode</li><li>Doppler Mode</li><li>Pulse Inversion Mode</li><li>Harmonic Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound machines are available in various types to cater to different medical imaging needs. A-Mode is the simplest form providing information on depth and size of structures. B-Mode or 2D Mode displays a two-dimensional image of organs or tissues. C-Mode allows for continuous scanning in a specific direction. M-Mode shows movement over time. Doppler Mode measures blood flow. Pulse Inversion Mode enhances image quality. Harmonic Mode improves resolution by reducing artifacts. Each market type offers specific features for precise imaging in medical diagnostics.</p></p>
