<p><ul><li>H-Gearbox</li><li>Sequential Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorsport transmission market features two main types: H-Gearbox and Sequential Gearbox. The H-Gearbox is a traditional manual gearbox with an H-pattern shifter, allowing drivers to manually select gears by moving the shifter in an H-shaped pattern. On the other hand, the Sequential Gearbox is a type of gearbox where gears are selected in sequence, typically using a lever or paddle shifters. The Sequential Gearbox offers faster and more precise gear changes compared to the H-Gearbox, making it popular in high-performance racing vehicles.</p></p>
