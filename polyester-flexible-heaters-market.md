<p><strong>The Polyester Flexible Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Etched Foil Polyester Flexible Heaters</li><li>Wire Wound Polyester Flexible Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester flexible heaters come in two main types: etched foil and wire wound. Etched foil heaters are made by etching a resistive circuit onto a thin polyester film, providing even heat distribution. These heaters are flexible, lightweight, and have a fast response time. Wire wound heaters, on the other hand, consist of a resistive wire wound around a fiberglass core and are generally more durable and robust than etched foil heaters. Both types are commonly used in a variety of industries for applications requiring flexible and efficient heating solutions.</p></p>
