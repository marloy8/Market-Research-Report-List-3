<p><p>Submarine fiber optic cables can be classified into two main types: single core and multicore. Single core cables consist of a single optical fiber for transmitting data, while multicore cables have multiple fibers for increased capacity and redundancy. Single core cables are typically used for smaller capacity connections, while multicore cables are used for larger capacity and more resilient undersea communication networks. Both types play a crucial role in enabling high-speed internet connectivity between countries and continents.</p></p>
