Light Capacity lever hoists are designed for small lifting tasks, typically with a maximum load of up to 1 ton. Standard Capacity lever hoists are suitable for medium-sized lifting applications, with a load capacity ranging from 1 to 3 tons. Heavy Capacity lever hoists are built to handle large and heavy loads, with a capacity exceeding 3 tons. Each market type caters to different lifting needs based on load requirements.</p></p>
