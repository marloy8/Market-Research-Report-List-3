<p><p>Electric tube furnaces are available in two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal tube furnaces are designed for applications where materials need to be heated evenly in a horizontal position. They are commonly used in industrial processes such as sintering and annealing. Vertical tube furnaces, on the other hand, are designed for applications where materials need to be heated vertically. They are often used in industries like semiconductor manufacturing and research laboratories for processes like CVD and crystal growth.</p></p>
