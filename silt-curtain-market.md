<p><p>Silt curtain market types include woven fibers and non-woven fibers. Woven fiber silt curtains are made by weaving fabric strands together, creating a strong and durable barrier. Non-woven fiber silt curtains, on the other hand, are made by bonding fibers together through heat or chemicals, offering a cost-effective solution for sediment control. Both types of silt curtains serve the same purpose of containing sediment and preventing erosion, with woven fibers providing more durability and non-woven fibers offering a more budget-friendly option.</p></p>
