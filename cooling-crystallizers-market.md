<p><p>Cooling crystallizers are categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal cooling crystallizers are designed with heat exchange tubes running horizontally, allowing for a more uniform cooling process and ideal for large-scale production. Vertical cooling crystallizers, on the other hand, have heat exchange tubes running vertically, making them suitable for smaller batches and processes requiring precise temperature control. Both types of cooling crystallizers play a vital role in various industries to facilitate the crystallization of substances.</p></p>
