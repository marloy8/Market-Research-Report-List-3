<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hole Transport Layer Materials (HTL)</li><li>Electron Transport Layer Materials (ETL)</li><li>Electrode materials</li><li>Emissive Layer Materials (EML)</li><li>Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED market consists of various types of materials, including Hole Transport Layer (HTL) materials, Electron Transport Layer (ETL) materials, Electrode materials, Emissive Layer Materials (EML), and Substrates. HTL materials facilitate the movement of positively charged holes, while ETL materials help the movement of negatively charged electrons within the OLED structure. Electrode materials are used to inject and extract charges from the device. EML materials emit light when charges recombine, and substrates provide structural support for the OLED device.</p></p>
