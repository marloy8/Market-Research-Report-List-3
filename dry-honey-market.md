<p><p>Dry honey market types include granulated honey and powdered honey. Granulated honey is a combination of crystallized honey and dry sweeteners, giving it a sandy texture suitable for baking and cooking. Powdered honey, on the other hand, is finely ground honey that can easily dissolve in liquids. Both products offer convenience and versatility in adding honey flavor to various dishes without the sticky mess of traditional honey. These dry honey varieties are popular among consumers looking for easy ways to incorporate honey into their recipes.</p></p>
