<p><p>Pneumatic conveying equipment market includes Positive Pressure Conveying, Vacuum Pressure Conveying, and Combined Conveying types. Positive Pressure Conveying systems push materials through a pipeline using a blower or fan. Vacuum Pressure Conveying systems use suction to pull materials through the pipeline. Combined Conveying systems utilize both positive and vacuum pressures for efficient material transfer. These types offer various advantages such as minimal material degradation, dust-free operation, and flexibility in conveying different materials.</p></p>
