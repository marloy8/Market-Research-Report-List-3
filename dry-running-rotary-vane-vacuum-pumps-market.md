<p><p>Dry running rotary vane vacuum pumps are classified into two types - single stage and two stage. Single stage pumps have a simple design with one rotor and are suitable for applications where a lower vacuum level is required. Two stage pumps have two rotors, which allow for a higher vacuum level to be achieved. They are commonly used in applications where a deeper vacuum is needed, such as in industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
