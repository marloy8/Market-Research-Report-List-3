<p><p>The Automotive Tie Rod Assembly market consists of two main types: Steering Tie Rod Assembly and Straight Tie Rod Assembly. Steering Tie Rod Assembly is used in vehicles to transmit force from the steering center link to the steering knuckle, allowing for precise steering control. On the other hand, Straight Tie Rod Assembly is used to connect the steering system with the wheels, helping to ensure proper alignment and stability. Both types play a crucial role in the overall performance and functionality of a vehicle's steering system.</p></p>
