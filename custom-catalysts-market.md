<p><p>Custom catalysts can be categorized into two main types: synthetic catalysts and natural catalysts. On the other hand, natural catalysts are derived from living organisms or natural materials, offering a more sustainable and environmentally-friendly option. Both types of catalysts play crucial roles in various industrial processes, helping to increase the efficiency and selectivity of chemical reactions.</p></p>
