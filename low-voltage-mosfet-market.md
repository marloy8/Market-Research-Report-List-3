<p><p>N-Channel and P-Channel are two types of MOSFETs commonly used in low voltage applications. N-Channel MOSFETs are more popular due to their higher electron mobility and faster switching speeds. They are preferred in applications where efficiency and high performance are crucial. P-Channel MOSFETs, on the other hand, have lower electron mobility but are easier to control and require less circuitry. They are commonly used in applications where simplicity and ease of use are more important than speed and efficiency.</p></p>
