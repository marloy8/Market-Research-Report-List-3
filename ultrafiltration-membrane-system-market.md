<p><p>The Ultrafiltration Membrane System market can be categorized into two types based on the material used in the membrane: Inorganic Membrane and Organic Membrane. Inorganic membranes are made of materials like ceramics, metals, or carbon, offering high chemical and thermal resistance. Organic membranes, on the other hand, are made of polymers like polyethersulfone or polyvinylidene fluoride, providing flexibility and ease of fabrication. Both types offer different advantages and are used in various applications within the ultrafiltration membrane system industry.</p></p>
