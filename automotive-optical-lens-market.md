<p><strong>The Automotive Optical Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Lens</li><li>Aspherical Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spherical lenses are curved lenses with a uniform curvature, used in automotive headlights and cameras to focus light rays. Aspherical lenses have a more complex, non-uniform curvature that helps reduce spherical aberration and distortion in automotive optical systems. They are used in sensors, cameras, and navigation systems to improve image quality and enhance the overall performance of the system. Both types of lenses are essential in the automotive industry for ensuring clear visibility, accurate imaging, and efficient light management.</p></p>
