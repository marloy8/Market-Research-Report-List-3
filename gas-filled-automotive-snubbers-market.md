<p><ul><li>Single-Tube Snubbers</li><li>Twin-Tube Snubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas-filled automotive snubbers can be categorized into two main types: single-tube snubbers and twin-tube snubbers. Single-tube snubbers are designed with a single chamber for the gas and hydraulic fluid, providing a simple and compact solution for vehicle suspension systems. On the other hand, twin-tube snubbers have two separate chambers for the gas and fluid, allowing for better control of damping performance and heat dissipation. Both types of snubbers play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle stability, comfort, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
