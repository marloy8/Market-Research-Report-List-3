<p><p>Virgin polyester staple fiber can be classified into two main market types: solid fiber and hollow fiber. Solid fiber is made up of continuous filaments with a round cross-section, providing bulkiness and warmth to products like bedding and outerwear. On the other hand, hollow fiber contains empty cavities within the filaments, offering enhanced insulation properties for products like sleeping bags and jackets. Both types of fiber are widely used in various industries such as textiles, automotive, and home furnishings for their durability, moisture-wicking, and hypoallergenic properties.</p></p>
