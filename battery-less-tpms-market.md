<p><ul><li>SAW-based Technology</li><li>Electromagnetic Close-Coupling Technology</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Battery-less TPMS technology eliminates the need for regular maintenance and replacement of batteries in tire pressure monitoring systems. SAW-based technology uses surface acoustic wave sensors to measure tire pressure. Electromagnetic close-coupling technology utilizes electromagnetic waves to transmit data between sensors and the vehicle. Other market types include systems utilizing piezoelectric sensors, capacitive sensors, and micro-electromechanical systems (MEMS) technology. These advanced technologies offer long-term reliability, reduced cost of ownership, and improved safety by continuously monitoring tire pressure without the need for batteries.</p></p>
