<p><p>There are different types of touch screen displays in the market. Resistive type screens are pressure-sensitive and require physical contact to register input. Capacitive type screens use the electrical charge of the human body to detect touch. Infrared type screens use infrared light to detect touch, making them highly sensitive and accurate. There are also other types of touch screens, such as surface capacitive, acoustic pulse recognition, and optical imaging, each with its own unique features and applications.</p></p>
