<p><p>Cloud-Based SIEM market offers three types based on deployment: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Public Cloud SIEM involves services offered over the internet by third-party providers. Private Cloud SIEM is deployed solely for one organization and is managed either by the organization or a third-party provider. Hybrid Cloud SIEM combines both public and private cloud deployments, offering flexibility and customization. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences, ranging from shared resources in public cloud to dedicated resources in private cloud.</p></p>
