<p><p>Research slide scanners are used in the field of pathology and research to digitize glass slides for analysis. There are two main types of slide scanners: Brightfield and Fluorescence. Brightfield scanners are commonly used for routine tissue analysis, where the sample is illuminated from below and the image is captured using visible light. Fluorescence scanners, on the other hand, are specialized for samples that emit fluorescent light when excited by a specific wavelength of light, allowing for the visualization of specific markers in the tissue.</p></p>
