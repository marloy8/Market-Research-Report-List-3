<p><p>Rubber vulcanization is a chemical process that converts natural or synthetic rubber into a more durable and stable material. The market for rubber vulcanization additives can be classified into different types - accelerators, vulcanizing agents, activators, and others. Accelerators are compounds that speed up the vulcanization process, vulcanizing agents are responsible for cross-linking the polymer chains, activators enhance the effectiveness of accelerators, and other additives may include antioxidants, fillers, or plasticizers. Each type of additive plays a specific role in improving the overall properties of the rubber product.</p></p>
