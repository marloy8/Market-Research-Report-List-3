<p><p>Ceramic chucks market can be categorized into three main types based on the material used: Al2O3 (aluminum oxide), AlN (aluminum nitride), and SiC (silicon carbide). Al2O3 chucks are known for their excellent heat resistance and durability. AlN chucks offer high thermal conductivity and electrical insulation properties. SiC chucks are preferred for their superior hardness and wear resistance. Each type of ceramic chuck caters to specific industries and applications, providing unique advantages for different manufacturing processes.</p></p>
