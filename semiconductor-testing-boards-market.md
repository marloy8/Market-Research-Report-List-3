<p><p>Semiconductor testing boards, such as ProbeCards, LoadBoards, and Burn-inBoards, serve different functions in the semiconductor testing process. ProbeCards are used to establish electrical connections for testing integrated circuits. LoadBoards provide a platform for connecting the device under test to the test system. Burn-inBoards facilitate the testing of semiconductor devices under harsh conditions to identify potential failures. Each type of board plays a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor products.</p></p>
