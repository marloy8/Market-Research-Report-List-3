<p><p>Prepaid gift cards are categorized into two main types: open loop and closed loop markets. Open loop gift cards are typically issued by major credit card companies and can be used at a wide range of merchants. Closed loop gift cards, on the other hand, are tied to a specific retailer or group of retailers and can only be used at those locations. Each type offers unique advantages and restrictions for both consumers and businesses looking to purchase or accept gift cards.</p></p>
