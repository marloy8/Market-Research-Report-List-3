<p><p>Walk-through Metal Detection Market includes LC Oscillating Metal Detector and Pulse Induction (PI) Metal Detector types. LC Oscillating Metal Detectors use an LC circuit to create an oscillating magnetic field that detects metal objects, while Pulse Induction Metal Detectors use pulses of current to create a magnetic field and measure the reflections caused by metal objects. Both types are commonly used in security settings such as airports, stadiums, and government buildings to screen individuals for concealed metal objects.</p></p>
