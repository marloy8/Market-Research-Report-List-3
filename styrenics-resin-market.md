<p><p>Styrenics Resin Market Types include GPPS (General Purpose Polystyrene), HIPS (High Impact Polystyrene), SAN (Styrene Acrylonitrile), and ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) resins. GPPS is commonly used for disposable packaging and household items, while HIPS is known for its impact resistance and is used in products like refrigerator liners. SAN resin offers a balance of properties such as clarity and heat resistance, making it suitable for consumer goods. ABS resin is a versatile material known for its strength and toughness, commonly found in automotive parts and electronics.</p></p>
