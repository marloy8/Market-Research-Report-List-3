<p><p>UV Laser Diode Market is categorized into two types: Single Mode and Multi-mode. Single Mode laser diodes emit light in a single spatial mode, providing a narrow and focused beam suitable for precision applications such as micro-machining or medical procedures. On the other hand, Multi-mode laser diodes emit light in multiple spatial modes, producing a broader beam that is ideal for applications like material processing or sensing. Each type caters to specific industry needs and demands.</p></p>
