<p><p>Ambient lighting refers to the general lighting present in a space to create a comfortable and inviting atmosphere. The market for ambient lighting includes various types such as recessed lights, surface-mounted lights, suspended lights, track lights, and strip lights. Recessed lights are fixtures that are installed into the ceiling for a seamless look. Surface-mounted lights are attached to the surface of the ceiling or wall. Suspended lights hang from the ceiling. Track lights are adjustable fixtures mounted on a track. Strip lights are flexible and can be installed in various locations.</p></p>
