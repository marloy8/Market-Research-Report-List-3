<p><p>Vacuum gauge balls are tools used to measure pressure levels within vacuum systems. There are several types of vacuum gauges including Shultz Gauge, B-A Gauge, Nude Ion Gauge, Pirani Gauge, and Thermocouple Gauge. Shultz Gauges are known for their accuracy and reliability, while B-A Gauges are widely used for their simplicity. Nude Ion Gauges measure pressure by ionizing gas particles, Pirani Gauges measure pressure based on thermal conductivity, and Thermocouple Gauges utilize the Seebeck effect to measure pressure. Each type of vacuum gauge has its own unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
