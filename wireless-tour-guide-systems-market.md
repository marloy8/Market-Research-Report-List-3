<p><ul><li>Digital Signal Transmission</li><li>Analog Signal Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless tour guide systems are available in two main market types: Digital Signal Transmission and Analog Signal Transmission. Digital signal transmission systems offer higher clarity, range, and security compared to analog systems. They use digital encoding to transmit audio signals, resulting in better sound quality and resistance to interference. Analog systems, on the other hand, transmit audio signals in their original form without encoding. While analog systems may be simpler and more cost-effective, they generally offer lower sound quality and are more susceptible to interference.</p></p>
