<p><ul><li>Frac Sand Proppants</li><li>Resin-coated Proppants</li><li>Ceramic Proppants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Proppants are used in hydraulic fracturing to keep fractures open and allow oil and gas to flow to the wellbore. There are three main types of proppants: Frac Sand Proppants, Resin-coated Proppants, and Ceramic Proppants. Frac Sand Proppants are the most commonly used and are made of high-purity sand. Resin-coated Proppants have a layer of resin to improve their strength and conductivity. Ceramic Proppants are the highest quality and most expensive type, made from sintered bauxite or kaolin clay.</p></p>
