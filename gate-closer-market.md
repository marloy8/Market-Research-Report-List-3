<p><p>Gate closers are devices used to automatically close gates after they have been opened. There are two main types of gate closers: spring gate closers and hydraulic gate closers. </p><p>Spring gate closers use a powerful spring to push the gate closed once it has been opened, providing a reliable and cost-effective solution for smaller gates. On the other hand, hydraulic gate closers use fluid pressure to control the closing of larger and heavier gates, offering a smoother and more controlled closing mechanism.</p></p>
