<p><strong>The Doctor Blade Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexo</li><li>Gravure</li><li>Offset Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Doctor blades are essential components in the printing industry, used in flexo, gravure, and offset printing processes. In flexo printing, doctor blades are used to control ink flow onto the printing plate. In gravure printing, doctor blades help to scrape excess ink off the surface of the cylinder. In offset printing, doctor blades are used to remove any excess ink from the printing plate. Overall, doctor blades play a crucial role in maintaining consistent print quality and maximizing efficiency in these printing applications.</p></p>
