<p><p>Car relay market types include Normally Open and Normally Closed relays. Normally Open relays have an open circuit when the relay is not energized, closing the circuit when power is applied. Normally Closed relays, on the other hand, have a closed circuit when not energized and open when power is applied. These different types cater to various automotive applications, providing flexibility and efficiency in managing electrical circuits within vehicles.</p></p>
