<p><p>Cermet potentiometers can be divided into two main types: single-turn and multi-turn. Single-turn cermet potentiometers have a simple design with one rotation for adjusting the resistance. They are commonly used in applications where precise adjustments are not required. On the other hand, multi-turn cermet potentiometers have multiple rotations for fine-tuning resistance levels. They are preferred in applications that demand a high level of accuracy and precision in resistance adjustments.</p></p>
