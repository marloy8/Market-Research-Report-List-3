<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Induction</li><li>Magnetic Resonance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive inductive wireless charging systems use electromagnetic induction or magnetic resonance technology to transfer power wirelessly to electric vehicles. Electromagnetic induction technology relies on creating a magnetic field between coils in the charging pad and the vehicle, inducing a current to charge the battery. Magnetic resonance technology allows for greater distance between the coils, enabling more efficient and flexible charging options. Both types of technology are being adopted in the automotive industry to provide convenient and efficient charging solutions for electric vehicles.</p></p>
