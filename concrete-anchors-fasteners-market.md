<p><p>Concrete anchors fasteners market can be categorized into two types - mechanical anchors or fasteners, and adhesive anchors or fasteners. Mechanical anchors are commonly used for heavy duty applications and rely on friction, expansion, or interlocking mechanisms to secure to concrete. Adhesive anchors use epoxy or chemical adhesives to bond with the concrete, providing a strong and durable connection. Both types of anchors have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
