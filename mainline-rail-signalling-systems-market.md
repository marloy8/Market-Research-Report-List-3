<p><strong>The Mainline Rail Signalling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Train Control</li><li>Communication Based Train Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traditional Train Control systems use fixed block signal systems with track circuits to maintain separation between trains. This type of system has been in use for many years and is still widely used in many parts of the world. </p><p>On the other hand, Communication Based Train Control systems use continuous communication between trains and the control center to determine train separation and movements. This type of system offers more flexibility and efficiency in managing train traffic, leading to improved capacity and reliability of the rail network.</p></p>
