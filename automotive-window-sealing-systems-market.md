EPDM is known for its durability and resistance to weathering, making it a popular choice for automotive seals. TPE offers flexibility and low compression set, making it suitable for sealing irregular surfaces. PVC is cost-effective and easy to process, making it a common choice for automotive window seals. Each material offers unique advantages to suit different automotive sealing needs.</p></p>
