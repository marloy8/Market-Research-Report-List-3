<p><p>Security window film market is categorized into three main types based on thickness: under 8 mil, 8 mil to 14 mil, and over 14 mil. Under 8 mil films are typically used for basic protection against shattering and break-ins. 8 mil to 14 mil films offer increased protection against forced entry and impact, making them ideal for high-security applications. Over 14 mil films provide the highest level of protection, often used in government buildings and high-security facilities where maximum security is required.</p></p>
