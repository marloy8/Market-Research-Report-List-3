<p><p>Zinc oxide surge arresters are commonly classified into two main types based on their coating material: composite coat type and porcelain coat type. Composite coat type surge arresters typically consist of a polymer-based outer layer, offering enhanced protection against environmental factors and ensuring longer lifespan. On the other hand, porcelain coat type surge arresters feature a ceramic outer layer, providing superior mechanical strength and durability. Both types are essential for protecting electrical equipment from overvoltage surges and maintaining the stability of power distribution systems.</p></p>
