<p><ul><li>Single-Len Wireless Endoscope</li><li>Dual-Lens Wireless Endoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless endoscopes come in two main types: single-lens and dual-lens. Single-lens wireless endoscopes have a single camera at the tip of the device, allowing for visual inspection of tight spaces like pipes or machinery. Dual-lens wireless endoscopes, on the other hand, have two cameras that provide a wider field of view and more detailed images. Both types offer the convenience of wireless connectivity, making them ideal for remote inspections in a variety of industries such as automotive, plumbing, and construction.</p></p>
