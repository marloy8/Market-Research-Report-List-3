<p><strong>The Air Diffusers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porous Plate Type</li><li>Porous Tube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air diffusers are devices used to evenly distribute air in a room or space. There are two main types of air diffusers - porous plate type and porous tube type. Porous plate type diffusers consist of a flat plate with numerous small holes that release air in a diffused manner. Porous tube type diffusers, on the other hand, consist of long tubes with tiny perforations that release air along the length of the tube. Both types are commonly used in HVAC systems to improve air circulation and distribution.</p></p>
