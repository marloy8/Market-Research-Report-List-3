<p><p>Hydrogenated oil market is divided into two main types - hydrogenated vegetable oil and hydrogenated animal oil. Hydrogenated vegetable oil is derived from plants such as palm, soybean, and sunflower, while hydrogenated animal oil is sourced from animals like cows and pigs. Both types of hydrogenated oil are commonly used in food processing for their solid consistency and longer shelf life. However, there has been a shift towards hydrogenated vegetable oil due to health concerns associated with saturated fats in animal-based oils.</p></p>
