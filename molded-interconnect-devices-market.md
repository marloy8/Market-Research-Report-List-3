<p><p>Molded Interconnect Devices (MID) market consists of various types including Laser Direct Structuring (LDS), Two-Shot Molding, and others. LDS technology involves directly writing circuit traces on injection molded components using lasers, enabling complex designs in small spaces. Two-Shot Molding combines two different materials in a single process, allowing for integrated functionality such as sealing or insulation. Other MID technologies may include conductive inks, plating, or additive manufacturing techniques to create unique interconnect solutions. Each type offers distinct advantages for different applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
