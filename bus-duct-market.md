<p><p>Bus duct market types include BMC (Air Splicing Bus Duct), CMC (Intensive Insulation Plug Bus Duct), and CFW (Higher Strength Enclosed Bus Duct). BMC utilizes air splicing for connections, providing flexibility and ease of installation. CMC features intensive insulation plugs for enhanced safety and reliability. CFW offers higher strength and is enclosed for added protection. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in industrial and commercial settings, offering various features and benefits to meet specific requirements.</p></p>
