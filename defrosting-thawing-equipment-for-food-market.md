<p><strong>The Defrosting & Thawing Equipment for Food Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chambers</li><li>Tumblers</li><li>Continuous Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Defrosting and thawing equipment for food are essential in the food industry to safely and effectively thaw frozen products. There are three main types of equipment used for this purpose: chambers, tumblers, and continuous lines. Chambers are large, enclosed spaces where frozen products are placed to thaw at a controlled temperature. Tumblers utilize a rotating mechanism to evenly thaw products. Continuous lines involve a conveyor belt system where products move through various temperature zones to gradually thaw. These different types cater to different production needs and capacities in the food industry.</p></p>
