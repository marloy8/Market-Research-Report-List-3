<p><p>GPS antennas for boats come in three main types based on their frequency range- Normal Frequency, High Frequency, and Ultra Frequency. Normal Frequency antennas operate in the 1-2 GHz range, providing standard GPS accuracy. High Frequency antennas operate in the 2-4 GHz range, offering improved accuracy and performance. Ultra Frequency antennas operate above 4 GHz, delivering the highest level of precision for navigation and tracking purposes. Each type caters to different needs and requirements of boat owners depending on the level of accuracy desired.</p></p>
