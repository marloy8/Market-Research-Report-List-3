<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Decanters</li><li>Floating Decanters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wastewater decanters play a crucial role in separating solids from liquids in the wastewater treatment process. Centrifugal decanters use centrifugal force to separate particles from liquids efficiently. Floating decanters utilize the principle of density difference to separate solids from liquids by allowing solids to settle at the bottom. Other types of decanters in the market include screw decanters and gravity decanters, which use different mechanisms to achieve the same goal of solid-liquid separation in wastewater treatment processes.</p></p>
