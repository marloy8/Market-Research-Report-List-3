<p><strong>The Handset Proximity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Displacement Sensor</li><li>Ultrasonic Displacement Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handset Proximity Sensor Market Types comprise Optical Displacement Sensor and Ultrasonic Displacement Sensor. The Optical Displacement Sensor uses light to detect the presence of an object, while the Ultrasonic Displacement Sensor emits ultrasonic waves and measures the time it takes for the waves to bounce back, determining proximity. Both sensor types are crucial for applications where precise distance measurement is essential, such as in smartphones for detecting when a user is holding the device close to their face during a call.</p></p>
