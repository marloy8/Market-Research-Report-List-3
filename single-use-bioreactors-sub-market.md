<p><p>Single-Use Bioreactors (SUB) are used in the biopharmaceutical industry for small-scale production of biological products. There are three main types of SUBs on the market: Wave-induced Motion SUBs, which use a rocking motion to provide oxygenation and mixing; Stirred SUBs, which use impellers for agitation; and Others, which include bubble columns and hollow fiber systems. Each type offers its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the production process.</p></p>
