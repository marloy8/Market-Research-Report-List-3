<p><ul><li>Wet Tile Cutting Machine</li><li>Dry Tile Cutting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tile cutting machines are classified into two main types based on their cutting mechanism: wet tile cutting machines and dry tile cutting machines. Wet tile cutting machines use water to cool the blade during cutting, reducing dust and preventing overheating. They are ideal for cutting harder materials like porcelain and stone. On the other hand, dry tile cutting machines do not use water and are suitable for cutting softer materials like ceramic and glass. Each type of machine caters to different cutting needs and materials.</p></p>
