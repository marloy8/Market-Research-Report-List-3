<p><p>Low signal relays are classified into two main types based on the type of signal they are designed to switch - AC signal and DC signal relays. AC signal relays are specifically designed to handle alternating current signals, while DC signal relays are meant to handle direct current signals. AC signal relays are commonly used in applications where the signal fluctuates in voltage and direction, while DC signal relays are typically used in applications where a constant voltage signal needs to be controlled or switched.</p></p>
