<p><p>Transcranial Magnetic Stimulator Market Types refer to different technologies used for non-invasive brain stimulation through magnetic fields. sTMS (single-pulse TMS) delivers one pulse at a time, pTMS (paired-pulse TMS) delivers two pulses in quick succession, rTMS (repetitive TMS) delivers repetitive pulses over time, and nTMS (navigation TMS) uses advanced imaging to precisely target specific brain areas. Each type has unique applications and benefits in the fields of neuroscience, psychiatry, and neurology for conditions such as depression, migraines, and neurological disorders.</p></p>
