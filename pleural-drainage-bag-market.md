<p><p>A Pleural Drainage Bag is a medical device used to collect and drain excess fluid from the pleural cavity, which surrounds the lungs. It is commonly used in conditions such as pleural effusion, pneumothorax, and empyema. The bag is attached to a chest tube that is inserted into the pleural cavity, allowing the fluid to drain out and be collected in the bag.</p><p>The Pleural Drainage Bag Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period.
