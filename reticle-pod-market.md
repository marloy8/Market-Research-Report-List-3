<p><strong>The Reticle POD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EUV</li><li>Non EUV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reticle pods are essential components used in the semiconductor industry for safely transporting and storing photomasks. There are two main types of reticle pod markets: EUV and Non-EUV. EUV reticle pods are specifically designed for masks used in Extreme Ultraviolet lithography, which is a cutting-edge technology used for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Non-EUV reticle pods, on the other hand, are used for masks in traditional optical lithography processes. Both types of reticle pod markets cater to different segments of the semiconductor industry based on the lithography technology being utilized.</p></p>
