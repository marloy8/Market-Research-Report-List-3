<p><ul><li>Self Generating Headlights</li><li>Battery Powered Headlights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bike headlights market includes two main types: self-generating headlights and battery-powered headlights. Self-generating headlights are powered by the motion of the bike and do not require batteries, making them eco-friendly and cost-effective in the long run. Battery-powered headlights, on the other hand, are powered by rechargeable or disposable batteries, offering a more convenient option for those who want a reliable source of light without relying on the bike's movement. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of cyclists in the market.</p></p>
