<p><p>Passenger Vehicle Gasoline Engine Intake Valve market consists of three main types: mono metallic valves, bimetallic valves, and hollow valves. Mono metallic valves are made of a single material, typically steel or titanium. Bimetallic valves consist of two different materials, usually steel and bronze or steel and aluminum. Hollow valves have a hollow stem, which reduces its weight and improves fuel efficiency. Each type offers unique benefits and is used based on specific engine requirements and performance goals in the automotive industry.</p></p>
