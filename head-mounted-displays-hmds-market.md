<p><p>Head-mounted Displays (HMDs) are available in three main market types - Slide-on HMD, Discrete HMD, and Integrated HMD. Slide-on HMDs are designed to attach to existing headgear, offering a cost-effective solution for users. Discrete HMDs are stand-alone devices that provide a high level of immersion but can be bulky. Integrated HMDs are built into helmets or goggles, offering a streamlined and lightweight design for seamless use. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences within the HMD industry.</p></p>
