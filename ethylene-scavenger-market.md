<p><p>Ethylene scavenger is a type of packaging technology used to absorb and eliminate ethylene gas produced by fruits and vegetables during storage and transportation. By removing ethylene gas, ethylene scavengers help to extend the freshness and quality of fruits and vegetables, reducing food waste and increasing product longevity.</p><p>The Ethylene Scavenger Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period.
