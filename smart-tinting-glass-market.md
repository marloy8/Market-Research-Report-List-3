<p><p>Smart-tinting glass technology is categorized into three main types - photochromic, electrochromic, and thermochromic. Photochromic glass changes opacity in response to UV light exposure, providing automatic tinting in sunlight. Electrochromic glass alters transparency with the application of an electric current, allowing users to control the level of tint. Thermochromic glass adjusts opacity based on temperature changes, offering energy-efficient shading solutions. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, providing customizable tinting options for various applications in architecture and automotive industries.</p></p>
