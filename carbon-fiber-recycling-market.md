<p><p>Carbon fiber recycling can be classified into two main types: chemical process and physical process markets. The chemical process market involves breaking down carbon fiber composites using chemicals to recover the original fibers for reuse in new products. On the other hand, the physical process market involves mechanical crushing and grinding of the composites to extract the fibers. Both markets play a crucial role in sustainability efforts by reducing the waste generated from carbon fiber materials production.</p></p>
