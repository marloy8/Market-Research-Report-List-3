<p><p>Wideband amplifiers can be categorized into driver amplifiers and power amplifiers. Driver amplifiers are designed to boost the signal from a source to drive the following stage while maintaining signal integrity and minimizing distortion. On the other hand, power amplifiers are used to amplify the signal to a level that can drive a speaker or antenna with high power output. Both types of amplifiers play crucial roles in different stages of signal processing and transmission, catering to the specific needs of various applications.</p></p>
