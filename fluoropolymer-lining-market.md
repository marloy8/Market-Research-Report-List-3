<p><p>Fluoropolymer lining is used in various industries to provide corrosion resistance and chemical inertness. There are different types of fluoropolymer linings available in the market, including PVDF, ETFE, ECTFE, FEP, PTFE, and PFA. Each type has its unique set of properties and applications.
