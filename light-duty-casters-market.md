<p><p>Swivel casters and rigid casters are two main types of Light Duty Casters in the market. Swivel casters have a 360-degree swiveling capability, allowing for easy maneuverability in various directions. They are ideal for applications where frequent direction changes are required. On the other hand, rigid casters only move in a straight line, providing stability and control. They are suitable for applications that require straight-line movement and heavy lifting. Both types of casters play a crucial role in enhancing mobility and efficiency in different industries.</p></p>
