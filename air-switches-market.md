<p><p>Air switches are devices used to control electrical equipment through the use of air pressure. There are two main types of air switches in the market - Bellows and Push Button. Bellows air switches consist of a a flexible container that expands and contracts to activate the switch. Push Button air switches on the other hand, require manual push to activate the switch. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, HVAC, and manufacturing for their reliability and safety features.</p></p>
