<p><p>Video Door Phones come in different types based on their connectivity technology. WiFi Video Door Phones use a wireless network to connect the doorbell camera to the indoor monitor or smart devices, allowing for remote access and control. On the other hand, DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) Video Door Phones use a secure wireless technology to ensure interference-free communication between the doorbell camera and indoor monitor. Both WiFi and DECT Video Door Phones offer convenient and efficient ways to monitor and communicate with visitors at the door.</p></p>
