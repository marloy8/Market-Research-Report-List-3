<p><strong>The E-Beam Evaporator Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-pocket e-beam Sources</li><li>Single Pocket e-beam Sources</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E-beam evaporator systems market can be divided into two main types based on the number of pockets in the e-beam source: multi-pocket e-beam sources and single pocket e-beam sources. Multi-pocket e-beam sources have multiple pockets for holding different materials, allowing for simultaneous deposition of multiple layers with different compositions. On the other hand, single pocket e-beam sources have only one pocket, making them suitable for simpler deposition processes where only one material is needed.</p></p>
