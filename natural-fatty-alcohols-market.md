<p><p>Natural fatty alcohols can be classified into two main types: saturated alcohols and unsaturated alcohols. Saturated alcohols are derived from natural sources like coconut oil and palm oil, and are widely used in various industries including personal care, pharmaceuticals, and food. On the other hand, unsaturated alcohols are derived from sources like soybean oil and have different chemical properties compared to saturated alcohols. Both types of alcohols have unique characteristics that make them suitable for different applications in the market.</p></p>
