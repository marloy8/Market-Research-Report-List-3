Crystal membrane electrodes use a crystal membrane as the sensing element, while PVC membrane electrodes use a PVC membrane. Crystal membrane electrodes are known for their high selectivity and sensitivity, while PVC membrane electrodes are more cost-effective and easy to use. Both types of electrodes are widely used in various industries for monitoring sulphide ion concentrations.</p></p>
