<p><p>Pressed plastic-bonded magnets are classified into three main types based on the materials used in their production:</p><p>1. Rare Earth Bonded Magnets: These magnets are made with a combination of rare earth metals like neodymium and samarium, making them extremely powerful and strong.</p><p>2. Ferrite Bonded Magnets: These magnets are made with ferrite, a type of ceramic material, which is more cost-effective compared to rare earth magnets but still offers good magnetic properties.</p><p>3. Hybrid Bonded Magnets: These magnets combine both rare earth and ferrite materials to create a magnet with a balance of strength and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
