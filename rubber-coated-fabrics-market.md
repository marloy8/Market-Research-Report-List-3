<p><ul><li>Natural Rubber Coated Fabrics</li><li>Synthetic Rubber Coated Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber Coated Fabrics are categorized into two main types: Natural Rubber Coated Fabrics and Synthetic Rubber Coated Fabrics. Natural rubber coated fabrics are made from latex derived from rubber trees and are known for their high elasticity and resilience. On the other hand, Synthetic rubber coated fabrics are made from man-made materials such as neoprene or silicone, offering enhanced chemical resistance and durability. Both types of rubber coated fabrics are widely used in various industries for their protective and waterproof properties.</p></p>
