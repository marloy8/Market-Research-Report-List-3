<p><p>Engine lubricants market can be divided into two main types - single-grade and multi-grade. Single-grade lubricants have a specific viscosity rating and are designed for use in specific temperature conditions. Multi-grade lubricants, on the other hand, have a range of viscosity ratings and can adapt to different temperature conditions. While single-grade lubricants may offer better performance in extreme temperatures, multi-grade lubricants are more versatile and convenient for most applications. Both types cater to different needs in the automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
