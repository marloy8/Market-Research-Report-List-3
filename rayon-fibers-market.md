<p><strong>The Rayon Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscose Staple Fiber</li><li>Viscose Filament Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rayon fibers are classified into two main types: Viscose Staple Fiber and Viscose Filament Fiber. Viscose Staple Fiber is a short fiber form of rayon that is used to create textiles such as clothing, bedding, and non-woven products. Viscose Filament Fiber, on the other hand, is a long continuous fiber that is commonly used in the manufacturing of textiles like fabrics, carpets, and industrial products. Both types of rayon fibers have a wide range of applications in the textile industry due to their versatility and affordability.</p></p>
