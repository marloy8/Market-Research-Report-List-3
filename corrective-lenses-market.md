<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Corrective Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Divergent Lens</li><li>Convergent Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrective lenses markets are classified into two main types: divergent lens and convergent lens. Divergent lenses are designed to spread out light rays before they reach the eye, helping correct conditions such as myopia (nearsightedness). On the other hand, convergent lenses bring light rays together before reaching the eye, correcting conditions such as hyperopia (farsightedness). Both types of lenses help improve vision by focusing light properly on the retina, addressing different visual impairments and providing necessary vision correction for individuals with various refractive errors.</p></p>
