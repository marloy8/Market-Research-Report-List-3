<p><strong>The Light VehicleParking Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forward</li><li>Rear View</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forward parking sensors are installed in the front of a vehicle to help drivers park safely by alerting them of obstacles in front of the vehicle. Rear view parking sensors are installed in the rear of a vehicle to assist drivers in backing up safely by detecting obstacles behind the vehicle. Both types of parking sensors use ultrasonic technology to measure the distance between the vehicle and obstacles, providing audible or visual alerts to the driver to prevent accidents and damage while parking.</p></p>
