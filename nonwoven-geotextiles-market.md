<p><p>Nonwoven geotextiles are used in a variety of applications, and are available in different types based on the materials used in their manufacturing. PP (polypropylene) geotextiles are known for their strength and durability, making them ideal for applications that require high strength and permeability. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) geotextiles are known for their high resistance to chemicals and UV light, making them suitable for applications in harsh environments. PE (polyethylene) geotextiles are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for applications that require flexibility and ease of installation.</p></p>
