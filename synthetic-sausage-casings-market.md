<p><p>The synthetic sausage casings market includes different types such as collagen casing, cellulose casing, plastic casing, and others. Collagen casings are made from collagen derived from animal hides. Cellulose casings are made from plant-based cellulose. Plastic casings are made from materials like polyethylene. Other types of synthetic casings may include fibrous casings made from a combination of plant fibers and paper. Each type of casing offers unique advantages in terms of shelf life, cooking properties, and cost-effectiveness in the sausage production process.</p></p>
