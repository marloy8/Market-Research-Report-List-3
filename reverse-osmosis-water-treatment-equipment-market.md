<p><p>Reverse osmosis water treatment equipment market includes three main types: raw water pretreatment systems, reverse osmosis purification systems, and super purification systems. Raw water pretreatment systems prepare water by removing impurities before entering the reverse osmosis process. Reverse osmosis purification systems use a semipermeable membrane to remove contaminants. Super purification systems further enhance water quality by additional filtration processes. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring clean and safe drinking water for households, industries, and communities.</p></p>
