<p><strong>The Vibrating Fluid Bed Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Force System</li><li>Constant Displacement System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Constant Force System and Constant Displacement System are two types of vibrating fluid bed dryers in the market. Constant Force System utilizes a mechanism that maintains a consistent force on the material being dried, ensuring even drying and minimal product degradation. On the other hand, Constant Displacement System maintains a consistent displacement of the material, resulting in uniform drying and reduced energy consumption. Both systems are widely used in industries where precise control over drying process is essential.</p></p>
