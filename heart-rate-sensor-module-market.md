<p><p>The Heart Rate Sensor Module market can be divided into two main types: Photoplethysmography (PPG) Sensors Module and Electrocardiography (ECG) Sensors Module. PPG Sensors measure heart rate by detecting changes in blood volume through a light source, while ECG Sensors record electrical activity of the heart to calculate heart rate. Both modules are widely used in wearable fitness devices, medical monitoring equipment, and smartwatches to provide accurate and real-time heart rate data for users.</p></p>
