<p><ul><li>Star Targets</li><li>Sinusoidal Targets</li><li>Variable Frequency Targets</li><li>USAF Pattern Wheel Target</li><li>Resolving Power Chart</li><li>IEEE Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resolution test targets are used in the imaging industry to measure the resolving power of a camera or lens. Star targets have fine lines radiating from a center point, while sinusoidal targets have varying contrast levels. Variable frequency targets have alternating light and dark sections. USAF Pattern Wheel Targets have precise line patterns for resolution testing. Resolving Power Charts show the smallest discernible details in an image. IEEE targets are standardized for testing electronic displays. These targets cater to different industries and applications within the imaging and electronics markets.</p></p>
