<p><p>Car On-Dash Mounted Cameras are divided into Single-Channel and Dual-Channel market types. Single-Channel cameras record footage from one perspective, typically mounted on the front windshield. These cameras are commonly used for capturing road incidents and accidents. On the other hand, Dual-Channel cameras have two cameras; one facing forward and one facing the interior of the vehicle. These cameras are ideal for monitoring both the road ahead and the inside of the car simultaneously, providing additional security and surveillance features.</p></p>
